TYPEFACE

ASKEW is our in-house typeface, meticulously developed by designer Geordie Stevenson. The concept behind ASKEW was to explore the boundaries between regular and italic forms, blending elements of both into a single, cohesive design. By integrating the slanted, dynamic characteristics of an italic with the stability and structure of a regular font, Stevenson created a typeface that feels both expressive and grounded. The result is a distinctive, contemporary style that adds personality and visual interest without compromising legibility or clarity. ASKEW’s unique balance between traditional forms and unconventional angles makes it versatile for both print and digital applications, lending itself to a wide range of creative uses—from branding and editorial to web design and packaging.
